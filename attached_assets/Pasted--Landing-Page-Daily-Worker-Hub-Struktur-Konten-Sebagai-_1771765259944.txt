📖 How It Works (2 Paths)

Tabbed Interface: [Untuk Bisnis] | [Untuk Pekerja]

Path 1: Bisnis

1. Sign up — Verifikasi bisnis (5 menit)
2. Post job — Input posisi, tanggal, jumlah worker
3. Match & book — Filter by reliability, kirim request
4. Confirm & track — Worker accept, QR check-in
5. Auto-pay — Wallet system, transparan
Path 2: Pekerja

1. Create profile — Upload skill, foto, KYC (10 menit)
2. Browse jobs — Filter posisi, area, tanggal
3. Apply with tap — One-click, notif real-time
4. Check-in — Scan QR di lokasi
5. Get paid — Instant to wallet, withdraw anytime

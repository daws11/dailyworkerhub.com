/* Not your typical Inter/Poppins combo */

вҖў Headlines: Asymmetric alignment (not always left), break lines intentionally
вҖў Pull Quotes: Italic Playfair Display, left border with accent color
вҖў Numbers: Large display numerals for stats (e.g., "50+" in 4rem)

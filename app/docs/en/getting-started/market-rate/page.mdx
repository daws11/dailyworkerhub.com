# Market Rate Guide

## Standard Daily Rates by Position

Rates are approximate and may vary by location, experience, and demand.

| Position | Entry Level | Experienced | Senior |
|----------|------------|-------------|--------|
| Housekeeping | Rp 150K/day | Rp 200K/day | Rp 250K/day |
| F&B Service | Rp 150K/day | Rp 200K/day | Rp 280K/day |
| Kitchen Helper | Rp 130K/day | Rp 180K/day | Rp 220K/day |
| Bartender | Rp 180K/day | Rp 250K/day | Rp 350K/day |
| Receptionist | Rp 160K/day | Rp 220K/day | Rp 300K/day |
| Event Staff | Rp 150K/day | Rp 200K/day | Rp 280K/day |
| Cleaning | Rp 130K/day | Rp 170K/day | Rp 200K/day |

## Factors That Affect Rates

- **Location**: Bali, Jakarta, and Surabaya tend to have higher rates
- **Season**: Peak tourist season increases demand and rates
- **Experience**: Verified work history increases your market value
- **Certifications**: Food handling, safety, and language certifications add value

## Setting Your Rate

As a worker, you can set your own daily rate based on your experience and the market. Businesses will see your rate when reviewing applications.

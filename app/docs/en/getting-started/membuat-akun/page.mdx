# Create an Account

## Registration Process

1. Visit [app.dailyworkerhub.com/register](https://app.dailyworkerhub.com/register)
2. Choose your role: **Worker** or **Business**
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Enter your full name as it appears on your ID
6. Enter your phone number (WhatsApp)
7. Click **Sign Up**

## Email Verification

After registering, you'll receive a verification email. Click the link to verify your email address. If you don't see it, check your spam folder.

## Next Step

Continue to [Account Verification](/docs/en/getting-started/verifikasi-akun) to complete your profile.

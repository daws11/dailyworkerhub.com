# Account Verification

## Why Verify?

Verification builds trust between workers and businesses. Verified users get:
- Higher visibility in search results
- Access to more job opportunities
- Higher trust score
- Priority dispute resolution

## Verification Process

### For Workers
1. Upload a clear photo of your KTP or passport
2. Take a live selfie for face matching
3. Enter your bank account details
4. Wait for verification (typically 1-2 business days)

### For Businesses
1. Upload business registration documents (NIB, NPWP)
2. Verify business address
3. Provide bank account for escrow deposits
4. Wait for verification (typically 2-3 business days)

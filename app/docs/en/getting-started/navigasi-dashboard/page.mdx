# Dashboard Navigation

## Dashboard Overview

The Daily Worker Hub dashboard is your command center. Here's what you'll find:

### Navigation Bar
- **Jobs** — Browse and apply for available jobs
- **My Shifts** — View upcoming and past shifts
- **Messages** — Communicate with workers or businesses
- **Wallet** — Manage your funds and escrow transactions
- **Profile** — View and edit your profile settings

### Worker Dashboard
- Upcoming shifts calendar
- Earnings summary and history
- Job recommendations based on your skills
- Attendance and check-in status

### Business Dashboard
- Active job postings
- Worker applications and bookings
- Escrow fund management
- Attendance monitoring by location

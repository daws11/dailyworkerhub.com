# Protection Fund

## What is the Protection Fund?

The Protection Fund (_Dana Perlindungan_) is a community-funded safety net. 1% of every transaction contributes to the fund, which helps members in emergency situations.

### What It Covers
- Work-related accidents
- Payment delays from businesses
- Emergency assistance for verified members
- Partial compensation in unresolved disputes

### Community Managed
The fund is transparently managed by the community. All contributions and disbursements are publicly visible.

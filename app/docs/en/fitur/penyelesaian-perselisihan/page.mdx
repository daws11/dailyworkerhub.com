# Dispute Resolution

## Mediation Process

When a disagreement occurs between a worker and a business, our dispute resolution system provides a fair and structured process.

### Steps
1. **File a dispute** — Either party reports the issue within 24 hours
2. **Submit evidence** — Chat logs, photos, attendance records
3. **Mediation** — Our team reviews evidence and proposes a resolution (5 business days)
4. **Arbitration** — If mediation fails, an independent arbitrator makes a binding decision

### Evidence Requirements
- QR code check-in/check-out records
- Chat history within the platform
- Photos of completed work (if applicable)
- Any other relevant documentation

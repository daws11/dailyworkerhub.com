# Escrow System

## How It Works

The Escrow System (_Sistem Dana Jaminan_) protects both workers and businesses by holding payment until work is completed and confirmed.

### Flow
1. **Business deposits** funds before the shift starts
2. **Worker checks in** via QR code at the location
3. **Worker completes** the shift and checks out
4. **Business confirms** completion within 24 hours
5. **Funds are released** to the worker's wallet

### Disputes
If there's a disagreement, funds remain in escrow until the dispute is resolved through our mediation process.

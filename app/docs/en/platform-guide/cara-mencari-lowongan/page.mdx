# How to Find Jobs

## Browsing Available Jobs

1. Go to **Jobs** from the dashboard
2. Use filters to narrow results:
   - **Position** — Housekeeping, F&B, Kitchen, etc.
   - **Location** — Area within the city
   - **Date** — When you're available
   - **Pay rate** — Minimum daily rate
3. Click on any job to see details

## Applying

1. Review the job description, requirements, and pay
2. Click **Apply** — no need to send messages
3. The business reviews your profile and work history
4. If accepted, you'll receive a notification

## Tips
- Keep your profile complete and up-to-date
- A verified profile gets more applications accepted
- Check the business's rating before applying

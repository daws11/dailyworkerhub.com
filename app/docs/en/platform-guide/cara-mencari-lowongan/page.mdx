# How to Search for Jobs

Daily Worker Hub connects you directly with hospitality businesses in Bali — without intermediaries, without hidden fees. This guide explains in detail how to find, apply, and manage daily job listings effectively.

---

## Step 1: Login and Access the Dashboard

Open the Daily Worker Hub app and login with your registered email and password. You will go directly to the dashboard which displays:

| Component | Description |
|----------|-----------|
| **Latest Job Listings** | List of new job listings posted in the last 24 hours, tailored to your location and preferences |
| **Application Status** | Summary of active applications: Pending, Accepted, Expired |
| **Notifications** | Messages from employers, application status updates, and platform announcements |
| **Quick Stats** | Number of applications today, total applications, and your rating |

---

## Step 2: Use Search Filters

Filters are key to finding the right job listings quickly. Use the following filters to narrow search results:

### Main Filters

| Filter | How to Use | Tips |
|--------|-----------------|------|
| **Location** | Select an area in Bali (Seminyak, Kuta, Ubud, Canggu, Sanur, Jimbaran, Nusa Dua) or use a 1-10km radius from your GPS | Use a 5km radius if you have your own transportation; use a specific area if you can only be in one location |
| **Job Type** | Choose from: Bartender, Waiter, Kitchen, Housekeeping, Receptionist, Driver, Barista, Chef, Events, or "All" | Choose specific if you have a particular skill; choose "All" to see all opportunities |
| **Daily Rate** | Enter minimum and maximum pay range per day (e.g., Rp 150,000 – Rp 300,000) | Match your skills and experience. Don't go too low (undervalued) or too high (hard to get hired) |
| **Schedule** | Select work days: Weekdays, Weekends, or specific dates | This filter is very important to ensure your schedule is available |
| **Start Date** | Filter job listings that need workers starting on a specific date | Use this filter to plan work for the coming days |

### Sort Results

| Sort Option | Use When |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Newest** | Want to see the most recently posted job listings |
| **Nearest** | Want to find work closest to your location |
| **Highest Pay** | Want to see the best earning opportunities |
| **Most Popular** | Want to see the most applied-to job listings |

> **Tips:** Use **"Sort: Newest"** in the morning to see job listings posted overnight. Employers often post in the evening for the next morning's shift.

### Job Notification Feature

Enable **"New Job Notifications"** so you don't miss out:
1. Open **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Enable **"Job Notifications"**
3. Set preferences: location, job type, minimum rate
4. The system will send push notifications every time a new matching job listing appears

## Step 3: Read Job Descriptions Carefully

Before applying, make sure you understand every detail in the job listing description. Many failed applications happen because workers don't read the description carefully.

### Important Elements in Descriptions

| Element | What to Check | Ask If Unclear |
|--------|------------------|----------------------|
| **Tasks and Responsibilities** | What is specifically expected of you | "What specific tasks should I do?" |
| **Exact Location** | Full address or cluster area (visible after applying or chatting with employer) | "Where exactly is the location?" |
| **Work Schedule** | Start time, end time, shift duration, and work days | "What time should I arrive?" |
| **Daily Rate** | Pay amount, whether meals/transport are included | "Does the rate include meals and transport?" |
| **Special Requirements** | Certificates, experience, language skills, dress code | "Are there any special certificates required?" |
| **Work Duration** | How many days/weeks the work lasts | "How long does this work last?" |
| **Payment Method** | Payment via Security Deposit or direct | "What is the payment method?" |

> **Warning:** Do not apply to job listings that do not use the Security Deposit System. Direct payment without Security Deposit is not protected by the Protection Fund.

## Step 4: Optimize Your Profile Before Applying

A complete and professional profile significantly increases your chances of being hired. Employers are more likely to accept workers with complete profiles.

### Profile Optimization Checklist

| Element | Status | Description |
|--------|--------|------------|
| **Profile Photo** | ✅ Required | Clear face, friendly smile, neutral background. Avoid mirror selfies, dark photos, or photos with sunglasses. |
| **Full Name** | ✅ Required | As per KTP, not a nickname. |
| **Self Description** | ✅ Required | Write 2-3 sentences: main skills, hospitality experience, and availability. Example: "Experienced waiter with 3 years in 5-star hotels. Available weekdays and weekends. Fluent in Indonesian and English." |
| **Main Skills** | ✅ Required | Choose from the list: Bartender, Waiter, Kitchen, Housekeeping, Receptionist, Driver, Barista, Chef. |
| **Schedule Availability** | ✅ Required | Update every week — deactivate if not available. |
| **Location Area** | ✅ Required | Choose the area closest to where you live. |
| **Work Experience** | ✅ Highly Recommended | Business name, position, duration of work. |
| **Certificates/Documents** | ✅ Recommended | Upload health certificate, barista, food handler, etc. |
| **Portfolio** | ✅ Recommended | Photos of your best work (especially for chef, bartender, barista positions). |

### Tips to Increase Profile Visibility

| Tip | Explanation |
|------|------------|
| **Use skill keywords** | Include keywords in your description: "Bartender with cocktail and mixology experience", "Fine dining restaurant waiter", "Kitchen helper experienced in mise en place" |
| **Update availability regularly** | Workers who update availability regularly appear higher in employer search results |
| **Respond to chat quickly** | Fast response rate improves your profile ranking |
| **Get positive ratings** | Every well-completed job improves your profile in the eyes of other employers |

## Step 5: Apply and Wait for Response

After finding a suitable job listing, follow these steps:

### Application Process

1. **Click the "Apply" button** on the desired job listing
2. **Write a short message** (optional but highly recommended) — why you are suitable for this position
3. **Employer receives a notification** about your application
4. **Monitor status** in the **"My Applications"** menu

### Understanding Application Status

| Status | Meaning | Actions You Can Take |
|--------|------|------------------------|
| **Pending** | Application sent, employer is reviewing | Wait 1-24 hours. If more than 48 hours, try applying to other job listings |
| **Accepted** | Employer has accepted you as a worker | Confirm schedule, prepare yourself, and arrive on time |
| **Rejected** | Application not suitable or position already filled | Don't be discouraged — keep applying to other similar listings |
| **Expired** | Job listing has been closed by the employer | Similar job listings may appear again |

## How to Handle Communication with Employers

When an employer contacts you or you need to contact an employer, observe the following communication etiquette:

### Communication Etiquette

| Practice | Explanation |
|---------|------------|
| **Quick response** | Reply within 1-2 hours if possible. Fast response rate improves your rating |
| **Professional** | Use polite and clear language. Avoid slang or overly informal language |
| **Prepare questions** | Ask unclear details: dress code, meeting point location, arrival time, etc. |
| **Confirm attendance** | If you accept an offer, confirm clearly so the employer can plan |
| **Keep communication on platform** | Save chats on the platform. Don't communicate outside the platform before the transaction is complete |

### Examples of Effective Messages

**When Applying:**
> "Good afternoon, I am interested in the Waiter position for the night shift. I have 2 years of experience as a waiter at a 4-star hotel in Seminyak. I am available for the listed dates. Thank you."

**When Employer Contacts You:**
> "Thank you for contacting me. I can attend at the agreed time. May I know the dress code and meeting point location? Thank you."

**When Confirming:**
> "I confirm I can attend on [date] at [time] at [location]. I will arrive 15 minutes early. Thank you."

> **Note:** Never agree to direct payment (outside the Security Deposit). Payments outside the platform are not protected by the Protection Fund.

## Tips to Increase Chances of Being Hired

| Strategy | Explanation |
|----------|------------|
| **Apply in the morning** | Employers often check applications in the morning for same-day shifts. Best time: 06:00-08:00 WITA |
| **Don't wait for perfect** | Apply first, then you can get an opportunity. Applications not sent won't be accepted |
| **Upgrade skills** | Barista Certificate, Food Handler, or English language skills significantly increase your value |
| **Build reputation** | Do good work every time to get positive ratings from employers |
| **Be consistent** | Actively check-in and apply regularly. Workers inactive for 30 days will appear lower in search results |
| **Reply to messages quickly** | Employers tend to choose workers who are responsive and communicative |

## Process After Being Hired

After an employer accepts your application, follow these steps:

### Checklist After Being Hired

- [ ] **Confirm schedule** — Make sure date, time, and location are clear
- [ ] **Ask about dress code** — Whether there is a uniform or specific dress code
- [ ] **Check location** — Make sure the location is accessible and you know how to get there
- [ ] **Prepare equipment** — If there are tools or documents to bring
- [ ] **Arrive on time** — Arrive 15 minutes early for a professional impression
- [ ] **Check-in on the platform** — Confirm attendance through the app upon arrival
- [ ] **Do good work** — Give your best results according to the job description
- [ ] **Submit "Work Completed"** — After finishing, confirm on the platform
- [ ] **Wait for employer verification** — Employer will verify within 1-24 hours
- [ ] **Receive payment** — Funds enter platform balance, then withdraw

---

## Handover Process After Being Hired

A good handover process ensures a smooth transition from interview to active work:

1. **Final Confirmation** — Chat with the employer 1 day before the work date to confirm attendance
2. **Prepare Documents** — Bring your original KTP (if requested) and relevant additional documents
3. **Arrive on Time** — Arrive 15 minutes early. If there is a delay, immediately notify the employer
4. **Check-In on Platform** — When you arrive at the location, open the app and click "Check-In" to record attendance
5. **Request Briefing** — Ask the employer to explain tasks and procedures in detail
6. **Work Professionally** — Follow instructions, ask if unclear, maintain a professional attitude
7. **Submit Completion** — After the shift ends, click "Work Completed" on the platform
8. **Request Feedback** — After the transaction is complete, check the review from the employer and give a review for the employer

## Job Search FAQ

**Q: How many job listings can I apply to in a day?**
A: Unlimited. You can apply to all job listings that match your criteria and skills.

**Q: Can I cancel an application after being Accepted?**
A: Yes, but it is highly recommended to avoid it. Canceling after being accepted will affect your rating and the employer may not accept you again.

**Q: What if I don't have hospitality experience?**
A: Many job listings are for entry-level positions. Prioritize applying to kitchen helper, waiter, or housekeeping positions. Experience is not required for entry-level positions.

**Q: Can I see employer reviews before applying?**
A: The employer review feature is under development. For now, check the job listing description and ratings of other workers who have worked there as a reference.

**Q: What should I do if the employer doesn't verify after the work is completed?**
A: Wait 48 hours, then send a reminder message. If there is still no response after 72 hours, file a dispute through the "My Transactions" → "File Dispute" menu.

**Q: What if the work location is different from what's listed in the description?**
A: Ask the employer via chat before going. If the location is significantly different (e.g., listed as Seminyak but actually in Nusa Dua), you have the right to refuse and file a dispute.

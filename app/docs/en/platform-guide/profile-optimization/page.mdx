# Profile Optimization

## Why Optimize Your Profile?

A complete, well-optimized profile:
- Appears higher in search results
- Gets more job application acceptances
- Builds trust with businesses
- Can command higher rates

## Worker Profile Checklist

- [ ] Professional profile photo
- [ ] Complete work history (positions, dates)
- [ ] Skills and certifications listed
- [ ] Verified identity (KTP/passport)
- [ ] Bank account linked
- [ ] Availability calendar up to date
- [ ] Brief bio describing your experience

## Business Profile Checklist

- [ ] Company logo uploaded
- [ ] Business registration verified
- [ ] Clear description of your business
- [ ] Payment method configured
- [ ] Past worker reviews visible (builds trust)

## Reputation Score

Your reputation score is based on:
- Completed shifts without issues
- Positive reviews from the other party
- Attendance and punctuality
- Communication responsiveness

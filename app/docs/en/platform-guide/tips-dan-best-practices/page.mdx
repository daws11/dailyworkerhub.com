# Tips & Best Practices

## For Workers

### Before the Shift
- Confirm your availability the day before
- Check the location and plan your commute
- Review the job requirements and dress code
- Charge your phone fully (needed for QR check-in)

### During the Shift
- Arrive 10-15 minutes early
- Check in immediately via QR code
- Communicate any issues promptly through the platform
- Be professional and courteous

### After the Shift
- Check out via QR code when finished
- Confirm completion
- Leave an honest review for the business
- Request your payment withdrawal

## For Businesses

### Before the Shift
- Confirm worker details and requirements
- Ensure escrow deposit is sufficient
- Prepare QR code for check-in station
- Brief any on-site supervisors

### During the Shift
- Monitor check-in/check-out via dashboard
- Address worker questions promptly
- Document any issues with photos

### After the Shift
- Confirm completion within 24 hours
- Leave an honest review for the worker
- Review and manage upcoming bookings

## Communication Best Practices
- Use the platform messaging system for all work-related communication
- Keep conversations professional and documented
- Report any inappropriate behavior immediately

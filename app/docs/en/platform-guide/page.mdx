# Platform Guide

Daily Worker Hub is a two-sided platform connecting **daily workers** with **employers** in the Bali hospitality sector. This page guides you in using the platform effectively, according to your account type.

---

## For Daily Workers

If you are a daily worker looking for daily jobs in the Bali hospitality sector, this guide explains how to find the right job listings, apply effectively, and get paid safely.

### Quick Start Steps

1. **[Create a Worker Account](/docs/en/getting-started/membuat-akun)** — Register and verify your account in 5 minutes
2. **[Complete Your Profile](/docs/en/getting-started/persiapan-dasar)** — Photo, description, skills, availability
3. **[Search for Jobs](/docs/en/platform-guide/cara-mencari-lowongan)** — Use filters to find suitable jobs
4. **Apply & Get Hired** — Apply and wait for confirmation from the employer
5. **Work & Get Paid** — Complete the work, funds disbursed via Security Deposit

### Key Features for Workers

| Feature | Description | Action |
|-------|-----------|------|
| **Job Search** | Filter by location, type, rate, schedule | Open the "Search Jobs" menu |
| **Apply for Jobs** | Apply in one click | Click "Apply" on the desired job listing |
| **Employer Chat** | Direct communication with employers | Open chat from notifications or application page |
| **Balance & Withdraw** | View work earnings balance and disburse to bank account | Open "My Balance" menu → "Withdraw" |
| **Ratings & Reviews** | View ratings from other employers | Open your profile |
| **Notifications** | Get alerts for new matching job listings | Enable notifications in settings |

### Effective Tips for Workers

- **Apply in the morning** — Employers often check applications in the morning for same-day shifts
- **Complete your profile fully** — Complete profiles appear higher in employer search results
- **Update availability** — Always update your schedule so employers know you are active and available
- **Build reputation** — Do good work every time to get positive ratings
- **Don't give up** — If one application is rejected, apply to other similar job listings

> **Learn more:** [How to Search for Jobs — Complete Guide](/docs/en/platform-guide/cara-mencari-lowongan)

## For Employers

If you are a hospitality business operator in Bali who needs daily workers, this guide explains how to post job listings, recruit the best workers, and manage payments safely.

### Quick Start Steps

1. **[Create an Employer Account](/docs/en/getting-started/membuat-akun)** — Register and verify your business
2. **[Deposit Balance](/docs/en/fitur/sistem-dana-jaminan)** — Top up balance for Security Deposit
3. **[Post a Job Listing](/docs/en/platform-guide/cara-posting-lowongan)** — Create a job listing with a complete description
4. **Screening & Hire** — Select the best workers from applicants
5. **Pay & Review** — Complete the transaction and give a review

### Key Features for Employers

| Feature | Description | Action |
|-------|-----------|------|
| **Post a Job** | Create a job listing with complete details | Open the "Post Job" menu |
| **Worker Database** | Search for workers by skills, location, rating | Open the "Search Workers" menu |
| **Worker Chat** | Direct communication with applicants | Open chat from the applicants page |
| **Deposit Security Deposit** | Deposit funds for payment guarantee | Open "Balance" menu → "Deposit" |
| **Approval & Release** | Verify work and release funds | Open active transactions |
| **Ratings & Reviews** | Give ratings for workers after completion | Open completed transaction page |

### Effective Tips for Employers

- **Clear and detailed description** — Workers who understand the tasks will work better
- **Realistic rate** — Match the complexity of the work and market rate
- **Quick response** — Applications not responded to within 24 hours typically lose worker interest
- **Verify promptly** — Don't delay verification — workers are waiting for payment
- **Give honest reviews** — Helps build a better community

> **Learn more:** [How to Post Jobs — Complete Guide](/docs/en/platform-guide/cara-posting-lowongan)

## Platform FAQ

**Q: Is Daily Worker Hub free for workers?**
A: Yes. Registration, searching, applying, chatting, and withdrawing funds are all free for workers. All fees are borne by the employer.

**Q: Can I change my account type from Worker to Employer?**
A: Currently, the account type change feature is not yet available. You need to register a new account with a different email.

**Q: What is the difference between Verified and Unverified employers?**
A: Employers who have completed business verification receive a "Verified" badge indicating that the business identity and legality have been confirmed. Workers trust verified employers more.

**Q: What are the fees for employers?**
A: Employers are charged a Platform Fee of 3.5% + Security Deposit Service Fee + Protection Fund Contribution of 1% of the total salary per transaction. There are no other fees.

**Q: Is there an exclusive contract?**
A: No. You are free to work for multiple employers simultaneously, as long as there is no schedule conflict.

# How to Post Jobs

## Creating a Job Listing

1. Go to **Jobs** → **Post New Job**
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Position** — Select from predefined categories
   - **Date & Time** — When workers are needed
   - **Number of Workers** — How many you need
   - **Daily Rate** — Per worker per day
   - **Location** — Where the work will take place
   - **Description** — Specific requirements or notes
3. **Deposit escrow funds** — Total amount for all workers
4. Click **Publish**

## Managing Applicants

- Review applicant profiles and work history
- Accept or decline applications
- Workers receive automatic notifications

## After the Shift
- Workers check in via QR code
- Confirm completion within 24 hours
- Funds are released to workers

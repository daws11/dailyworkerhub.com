# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### Is Daily Worker Hub legal and safe?
Yes. All transactions use our escrow system, and both workers and businesses must complete identity verification.

### How does payment work?
Businesses deposit funds before the shift. After the worker checks out at the location via QR code, funds are automatically released to the worker's wallet.

### What is the Protection Fund?
A community fund from 1% of each transaction, used for emergency assistance like workplace accidents, payment delays, or disputes. Transparently managed by the community.

## For Workers

### What are the platform fees?
Workers pay **0% commission** on wages. Only a 1% Protection Fund contribution is deducted.

### How do I get verified?
Upload your KTP, a live selfie for face matching, and your bank account details. Verification typically takes 1-2 business days.

### Can I set my own rates?
Yes. You set your daily rate, and businesses see it when reviewing applications.

## For Businesses

### What are the platform fees?
Businesses pay a **3.5% platform fee** + escrow service fee + 1% Protection Fund contribution. No subscription or top-up fees.

### How does dispute resolution work?
If there's a disagreement, our mediation team reviews evidence from both parties. Funds stay in escrow until resolution. Arbitration is available if mediation fails.
